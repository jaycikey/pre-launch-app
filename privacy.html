Privacy Policy

Short version: your budget is encrypted on your device with a passphrase only you know. We can't read it — and that's the point.

Effective July 18, 2026

What JayCK Flow is

JayCK Flow is a financial calendar. You enter incomes, bills, and plans yourself; the app projects your future balance. There are no bank connections, no imports of your credentials, and no advertising.

Data that never leaves your device unencrypted

Your budget — transactions, categories, balances, savings, debts — lives on your device. If you enable sync, it is encrypted on your device with a passphrase only you know before anything is uploaded. Our server stores an opaque encrypted blob plus a revision number and timestamps. We cannot decrypt it, and we cannot recover your passphrase. JayCK Flow is designed so that we cannot access your financial data.

Data we do store (only if you create an account)

  • Email address — your sign-in identity and where verification / password-reset codes are sent.
  • Password — stored only as a strong one-way hash (scrypt), never as text.
  • Sign in with Apple / Google — if you use them, we store the provider's account identifier and the email the provider attests. We never receive your Apple or Google password.
  • Subscription status — whether sync is active and until when, verified from the App Store transaction. Payments are processed entirely by Apple; we never see your card details.
  • Encrypted vault — the unreadable blob described above.

Using the app without an account stores nothing on our servers at all.

What we don't do

  • No analytics or tracking SDKs, no advertising, no selling or sharing of data.
  • No reading of your budget — our design gives us no technical means to.
  • No marketing emails. We email you only codes you request (verification, password reset) and replies to your support messages.

Security

All traffic uses TLS. On-device secrets (your session and passphrase) are kept in the iOS Keychain. Server-side, passwords are scrypt-hashed and session tokens are stored hashed and rotated. Our emails contain only short one-time codes — never links to click — and we will never ask for your password or passphrase.

Retention and deletion

You can delete your account from inside the app (Account & sync → Delete account). Deletion is immediate and permanent: the account record and your encrypted vault are removed from our servers. Data on your own device stays yours until you delete the app or its data.

Your choices

  • Use the app fully offline, with no account.
  • Export your data locally at any time from the app.
  • Delete your account and server data at any time, in-app.

Children

JayCK Flow is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted here with an updated effective date. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
